Rules

  • Stay on the CTF-GAME network while playing the CTF.
  • Targets are listed on the Score Board page.
  • Referees may change/add other rules at will.

Scoring Points

  • Create you team tag in the following HTML format: <team>Marion "Cobra" Cobretti</team>
  • Your goal is to deface the web page on each target with your team tag.
  • Once per minute (more or less) the scoring engine will see who currently has their team tag on the target, and score it.
  • Players will try to put up their own defacements, take down other player defacements, and lock down the target to keep other players out.
  • The team tag must be on the target for the scoring engine to register it, but the player can change the page however they like.
  • If no one changes your team tag, you will be awarded a point once per minute for that target. NetKotH = Network King of the Hill
  • Referees will work as a blue team to occasionally step in and change things on the targets. Fix the defacement, patch, roll back changes, etc., just to even the playing field and make things interesting.

Getting Started

  • The first step is to know your target. A good tool for recon would be Nmap. A good terminal command to use might be nmap ‐A 10.0.0.0/24. Nmap Cheat Sheet
  • Once your recon is done, lets search for an exploit that we can use against the target. A good terminal command to use might be searchsploit vsftpd backdoor. SearchSploit ‐ The Manual
  • Now you can attack. Metasploit is handy for this. A few good terminal commands to use might be msfconsole, then help. MSFconsole Commands, Metasploit Cheat Sheet
  • After the attack, you should have root access on the target. The Ultimate Command Cheat Sheet for Metasploit’s Meterpreter
  • Learn some vi editor commands, they will help you here. Vi Cheat Sheet 1, Vi Cheat Sheet 2
  • Keep learning.
