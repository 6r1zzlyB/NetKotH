Hardware Recommendations

  • A laptop that can boot from a USB drive.
  • A USB Wi‐Fi adapter / dongle. This will allow you to connect to the CTF game network and a 2nd wireless network that may have internet access. This 2nd network will allow you to lookup hints, guides, etc...
    • A few recommended adapters are the Panda PAU05, Panda PAU09 or ALFA AWUS036ACH.
    • A good resource to look at is wirelesshack.org.
  • A pico USB drive & format it with Kali Linux. This will allow you to boot to a Kali desktop from any computer. Using Kali as a daily driver is not recommended, since updating the distro can break functionality.
    • A few recommended USB drives are the Lexar JumpDrive S45 128GB & PNY Elite‐X Fit 64GB.
    • To make a Kali bootable USB drive, follow the instructions at the Kali Linux Official Documentation.
